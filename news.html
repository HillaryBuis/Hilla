News

The ISPSC Sta. Maria campus has a lot of courses and programs to offer. We have here the different kinds of courses and programs you can choose. We have also here great leadership. If you want more information just visit our ISPSC Sta. Maria Campus website just click the ISPSC Logo. Or click the ISPSC Sta. Maria official FB Page

College of Computing Studies is one of the department in ISPSC Sta. Maria Campus. There are two(2) programs that our department has to offer. We have the BSIT and BSIS programs. For more information about our department just click the logo and it will directly go to the official FB page of College of Computing studies.

Computing Society Of the Philippines is the Mandated organization of the students enrolled in College of computing Studies. CSP officers are elected every starts of the academic year. For more informations and activities of this student organization you can click the logo and it will directly go to the official FB page of CSP.

Supreme Student Government is the organization in the whole campus. It is the main org of all student in the whole ISPSC Sta. Maria Campus. For more information about the SSC Sta. Maria campus you may visit their official FB page by clicking the SSC logo.
